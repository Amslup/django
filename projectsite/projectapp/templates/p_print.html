Mania Magazine

Mania Magazine was an arts & culture magazine at UCSD that publishes once every quarter.

Design Leadership

As Layout Editor I oversaw everything related to design (page layout, typography, visual hierarchy). The Editor-in-Chief trusted me and gave me and my assistants complete control over the visual direction of the magazine. My team and I explored different ways to effectively organize the content and experimented how to make a magazine visually interesting while only being able to be printed in black and white.

My goal and philosophy was to elevate and enhance the writing pieces through my typography and graphic design knowledge. I wanted people on the team to feel proud that they were part of this and for the magazine to garner recognition on campus to attract more talent and readers.

Role

Design & Layout Editor

Design leadership, information hierarchy, page & layout design, and print specifications & hand-off.

The Loft

The University Events Office (UEO) had a performance lounge named "The Loft" – a venue and restaurant that produced over 200 concerts per year.

I produced original poster artwork for many musicians and designed a variety of Loft collateral including staff manuals, tri-fold brochures, venue signage, and merchandise.

Role

Jr. Graphic Design Assistant

Graphic design, page & layout design, and printing specifications

The front and back of a postcard design promoting the "Wonderland" festival from ArtPower (a program funded by UEO).
A postcard design advertising the Spark Party event for important patrons of ArtPower.
A poster advertising the venue locations and dates for Clara C, a musician.

Becky Bones

Becky Bones LLC. was an “edutainment” brand for kids and teens. They gave back 25% of profits to grassroots environmental solutions (initiatives kickstarted and led by teens in high schools) such as planting trees, starting recycling programs for their local communities, and other green projects.

Role

Illustrator & Graphic Designer

Illustration, character design, page & layout design.

Character Design

I worked with the CEO and co-founder of Becky Bones to create new characters for them. I was not the original artist, so when I began designing new characters I kept in mind the established visual style of the brand to make sure the new and old character designs would be cohesive.

The characters that I've made include: Nerdy Nate, Guzzler, Trashu, Batteron, Zippy, Can, Recycling Rex, Chemy, Cooler, H2Face, Bag, Mighty Lighty, the ninjas (Frank, Ed, WInston, and Ry), Spray, Drain, and Plastique.

Stickers of the Becky Bones characters were made to pass out at events.

Media Kit

I was in charge of assembling the Becky Bones media kit. I led the art direction, the page layout, and made all the accompanying graphics.
