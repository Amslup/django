Creating reusable page templates + establishing a design system

I worked with Marketing to build reusable layout templates to empower the team to quickly deploy and experiment with marketing content without bothering engineering.

Each section of these page templates were self-contained and therefore could be changed and updated as Marketing saw fit.

My colleague and fellow designer Mario Nikolic suggested the Atomic Design Methodology where we would break down elements of the web pages into discrete units and turn them into components to begin establishing a strong design system for RevOps.

Examples of Atom, Molecule, and Organism components. Atoms make up Molecules, which in turn make up Organism components.
